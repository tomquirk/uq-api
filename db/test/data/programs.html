I'm a domestic student

You are currently viewing content as a domestic student.

Change to I'm an international student

We offer tailored content, making it easier to find what you're looking for. If you're unsure, contact the UQ Student Centre for more information.

You're a domestic student if you are:

  • a citizen of Australia or New Zealand, or
  • an Australian permanent resident, or
  • a holder of an Australian permanent humanitarian visa.

You're an international student if you are:

  • intending to study on a student visa, or
  • not a citizen of Australia or New Zealand, or
  • not an Australian permanent resident, or
  • a temporary resident (visa status) of Australia.
